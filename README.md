## Why Use This

Meta Ads Manager is powerful, but pulling the exact data you need often means clicking through filters, adjusting date ranges, building custom reports, and exporting CSVs. This MCP server cuts through all of that.

**What you get:**

- **Instant campaign insights** — ask about performance, spend, ROAS, and creative metrics in natural language.
- **No setup complexity** — fully hosted, connects in 2 minutes, no Meta API app or access tokens required.
- **Multi-account support** — query multiple ad accounts from a single conversation, perfect for agencies.
- **Cross-platform context** — since this runs on Two Minute Reports, you can also pull in Google Ads, Shopify, GA4, and 22+ other sources in the same chat.

## What You Can Ask

### Campaign Performance

```
"Show me all active Facebook Ads campaigns with spend, results, and cost per result for the last 30 days"
"Which campaigns are delivering the most purchases this month?"
"Give me a daily performance breakdown for my prospecting campaign last week"
```

### Spend & Budget Analysis

```
"How much have I spent on Facebook Ads this month compared to last month?"
"Which campaigns are spending the most with the lowest ROAS?"
"Show me my daily spend trend across all campaigns for the past 60 days"
```

### ROAS & Revenue

```
"What's my overall Facebook Ads ROAS for Q1?"
"Which ad sets are profitable and which are losing money?"
"Show me purchase value trends week-over-week"
```

### Creative & Ad-Level Analysis

```
"Which ad creatives have the highest CTR this month?"
"Compare performance across my video ads vs. image ads"
"Show me the top 5 ads by conversion rate in my retargeting campaign"
```

### Ad Set & Audience Insights

```
"Break down performance by ad set for my top campaign"
"Which audiences are delivering the lowest CPA?"
"Compare Lookalike audiences vs. Interest-based audiences this month"
```

### Placement & Platform Breakdown

```
"How does performance compare between Facebook Feed and Instagram Stories?"
"Show me a placement breakdown for my best-performing campaign"
"Which platform (Facebook vs. Instagram) is driving more conversions?"
```

### Funnel & Engagement Metrics

```
"What are my link click-through rates across campaigns?"
"Show me ThruPlay rates for my video campaigns"
"Which campaigns have the highest landing page view-to-purchase rate?"
```

### Comparisons & Trends

```
"Compare my Advantage+ Shopping campaigns vs. manual campaigns for the last 30 days"
"How did my CPM change month-over-month this quarter?"
"Show me a trend line of impressions and purchases for the past 3 months"
```

### Visualizations

```
"Plot my weekly spend and ROAS as a chart"
"Create a bar chart comparing CPA across all campaigns"
"Visualize my conversion trend over the last 90 days"
```
